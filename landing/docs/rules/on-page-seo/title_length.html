Title is between 30-60 characters

medium

title_length

Last updated: 2026-06-23

Verifies the title tag is between 30 and 60 characters.

What does this rule check?

Search engines display approximately 50-60 characters of a title tag in results. Titles that are too short miss the opportunity to include relevant keywords and context. Titles that are too long get truncated with an ellipsis, cutting off important information.

Why is this important?

Titles that are too short lack context for search engines and users. Titles over 60 characters get truncated in search results, potentially hiding your brand name or key selling point. Optimal length improves click-through rate.

How to fix

Rewrite the title to be between 30-60 characters. Place the primary keyword near the beginning. Include a brief description and optionally your brand name separated by a pipe or dash.

Examples

Incorrect 
<title>Shoes</title>
<!-- Too short: only 5 characters, no context -->
Correct 
<title>Best Running Shoes for Marathon Training | ShoeHub</title>
<!-- 52 characters: keyword + context + brand -->
